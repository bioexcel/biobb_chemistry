31 * **rotexoh** (*bool*) - (False) allow existing OH & SH groups to rotate
32 * **allalt** (*bool*) - (True) process adjustments for all conformations (default)
33 * **onlya** (*bool*) - (False) only adjust 'A' conformations
34 * **charges** (*bool*) - (False) output charge state for appropriate hydrogen records
35 * **dorotmet** (*bool*) - (False) allow methionine methyl groups to rotate (not recommended)
36 * **noadjust** (*bool*) - (False) do not process any rot or flip adjustments
37 * **metal_bump** (*float*) - (None) [0~5|0.005] H 'bumps' metals at radius plus this
38 * **non_metal_bump** (*float*) - (None) [0~5|0.005] 'bumps' nonmetal at radius plus this
39 * **build** (*bool*) - (False) add H, including His sc NH, then rotate and flip groups (except for pre-existing methionine methyl hydrogens)
